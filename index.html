Personalise Your Experience

Every feature is designed to reduce cognitive load and improve comfort for neurodivergent users.

Dark Mode
Reduces eye strain with a fully adaptive dark theme that responds to your system preference or manual toggle — no harsh whites.
System default
Reduced Motion
Minimizes animations and transitions across the platform — respects your OS preference or can be set independently.
Motion reduced

Enhanced contrast ratios across all UI elements — fully certified to WCAG AA and AAA standards for visual clarity.
